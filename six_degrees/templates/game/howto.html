How to play Six Degrees!

  1. Click on new game.
  2. You will be presented with your start and goal articles.
  3. Click on the first node to reveal your next possible choices.
  4. Progress through the game, clicking node by node until you get to your goal!
Psssst! Here's an extra hint. You can zoom in and around the graph by using your mouse wheel, or pinching on mac!
